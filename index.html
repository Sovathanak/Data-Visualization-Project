Child Mortality vs Child Birth from 1950 to 2019

Child mortality seems to have greater numbers for countries in Africa and Asia than those in other regions. Child birth and Child mortality seem to have a relationship where if Child birth increase, so does the number of Child mortality.

Throughout the 69 years, Nigeria has lead in Child mortality per 10,000 and Child birth per 10,000 compared to countries with immense population such as India and China even though Nigeria's population, compared to these countries is rather small.
